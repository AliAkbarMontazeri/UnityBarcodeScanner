Unity Barcode Scanner

Since months, I was looking for a good way to parse QRCodes (and generic barcode) in Unity. On the Asset Store, few library are already providing that, but they are expensive, overly complex, not tested and not always maintained.

So, I just want to do something simple, readable, cross-plateform and open source.

  • Tested with Unity 5.3.x, 5.4.x
  • Tested on PC, Mac, Android, WebGL
